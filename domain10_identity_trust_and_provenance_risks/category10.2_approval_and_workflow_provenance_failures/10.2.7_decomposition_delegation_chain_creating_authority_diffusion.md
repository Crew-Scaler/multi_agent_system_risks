# 10.2.7 Decomposition Delegation Chain Creating Authority Diffusion [NoFW]

HTN hierarchical delegation creates diffusion of responsibility where no agent holds complete authority. Agents may claim authorization from upstream sources without verification, enabling malware propagating through delegation chains where each agent trusts upstream authority claims without independent validation.
