# 10.1.11 Streaming Attribution Spoofing Through Progressive Identity Claims [NoFW]

Streaming responses enable identity spoofing where early tokens claim identity ("I am the Security Agent") while later tokens execute malicious operations before verification can occur. Multi-agent dashboards displaying streaming with identity attribution are vulnerable because early tokens establish trust before the complete stream enables identity verification.
