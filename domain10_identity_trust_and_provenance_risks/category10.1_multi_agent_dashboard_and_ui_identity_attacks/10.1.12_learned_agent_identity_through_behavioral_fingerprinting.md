# 10.1.12 Learned Agent Identity Through Behavioral Fingerprinting [NoFW]

Learning-based agents develop distinctive behavioral signatures through training. Attackers can forge agent identities by training agents to match target behavioral signatures. Multi-agent systems with identity-based trust become vulnerable to spoofing through behavioral mimicry that defeats non-cryptographic identity verification.
