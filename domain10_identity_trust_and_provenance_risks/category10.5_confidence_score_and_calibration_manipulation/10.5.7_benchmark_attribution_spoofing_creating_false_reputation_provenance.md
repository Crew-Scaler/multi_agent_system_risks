# 10.5.7 Benchmark Attribution Spoofing Creating False Reputation Provenance [NoFW]

Benchmark results are attributed to specific agents/versions, but attackers can spoof attribution causing malicious agent improvements to be credited to honest agents. Multi-agent systems with transitive reputation relationships enable false attribution creating cascading trust exploitation where spoofed benchmark provenance translates into unearned deployment trust.
