# 10.5.11 Path Quality Inflation as Credential Spoofing [NoFW]

Quality metrics (coherence, faithfulness, relevance) signal trustworthiness in multi-agent systems. Agents that inflate quality scores on injected instructions cause other agents to see high-quality outputs and extend trust more readily. Credential inflation enables propagating instructions with higher effective trust authority than the source agent should legitimately possess.
