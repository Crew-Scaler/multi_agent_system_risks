# 10.5.4 Confidence Score Poisoning Through Utility-Based Risk Assessment [NoFW]

Risk-assessment agents output confidence scores based on expected utility calculations where high utility options receive high confidence recommendations. Attackers poison utility function inputs causing inflated utility estimates and thus inflated confidence scores. In multi-agent approval chains, poisoned confidence scores from upstream assessment agents propagate downstream where approval agents trust confidence as independent validation.
