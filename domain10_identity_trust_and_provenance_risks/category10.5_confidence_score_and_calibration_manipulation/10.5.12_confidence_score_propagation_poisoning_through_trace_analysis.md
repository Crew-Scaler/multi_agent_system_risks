# 10.5.12 Confidence Score Propagation Poisoning Through Trace Analysis [NoFW]

Confidence score propagation across agent boundaries creates an attack surface where manipulating traces showing confidence levels causes downstream agents to inherit false confidence signals. An attacker causing Agent A's confidence tracing to show fabricated supporting reasoning leads Agent B to propagate that false confidence forward as a solid premise. Unlike singular systems where confidence propagation remains internal, multi-agent confidence signals crossing boundaries enable confidence injection through trace manipulation affecting the entire downstream decision chain.
