# 10.5.9 Benchmark Leaderboard Manipulation as Agent Reputation Spoofing [NoFW]

Benchmarking creates leaderboards establishing reputation. Attackers exploit leaderboards by poisoning benchmark implementations showing malicious agents performing better than honest ones. Downstream agents selecting "best performing agents" preferentially choose poisoned agents, whose deployment data then further entrenches their apparent credibility in a self-reinforcing reputation cycle.
