# 10.6.7 Horizontal Scaling Creating Replica Identity Ambiguity [NoFW]

Replicas are identical copies making them interchangeable, and this identity ambiguity enables attackers to compromise one replica that remains indistinguishable from legitimate ones. Unique agent identities would enable anomaly detection; replica anonymity enables attackers hiding compromised instances within the fleet without triggering identity-based detection mechanisms.
