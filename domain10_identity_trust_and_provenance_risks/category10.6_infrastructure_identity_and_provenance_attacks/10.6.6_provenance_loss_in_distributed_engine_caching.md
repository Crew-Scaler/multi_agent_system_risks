# 10.6.6 Provenance Loss in Distributed Engine Caching [NoFW]

TensorRT engines cached on edge devices lack cryptographic bindings to build-time sources. An attacker with access to one edge device can replace the cached engine with a backdoored version. Multi-agent distributed caching creates independent provenance chains where compromising one cache poisons all agents at that location without any cryptographic mechanism to detect the substitution.
