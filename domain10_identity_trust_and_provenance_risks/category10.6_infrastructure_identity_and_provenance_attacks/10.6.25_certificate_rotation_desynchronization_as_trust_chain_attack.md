# 10.6.25 Certificate Rotation Desynchronization as Trust Chain Attack [NoFW]

Fleet Command uses automatically rotating X.509 certificates to authenticate edge agents. An attacker partially compromising the certificate authority can desynchronize certificate rotation across agents, creating windows where some agents accept certificates from malicious sources while rotation timing mismatches prevent synchronized revocation across the fleet.
