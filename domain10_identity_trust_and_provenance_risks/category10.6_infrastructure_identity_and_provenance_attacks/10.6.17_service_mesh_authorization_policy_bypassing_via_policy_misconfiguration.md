# 10.6.17 Service Mesh Authorization Policy Bypassing via Policy Misconfiguration [NoFW]

Kubernetes NetworkPolicy and Istio AuthorizationPolicy resources define which agents can communicate with which other agents. Misconfigured policies allow unintended agent-to-agent communication paths, enabling attackers controlling one agent to laterally move through misconfigured policies to directly inject instructions into other agents without expected authentication boundaries.
