# 10.6.15 Quantization Artifact Provenance Verification Gaps [NoFW]

Quantization artifacts (calibration data, quantized weights) stored in registries may lack strong provenance verification. Attackers inject artifacts claiming legitimate provenance, causing agents to load compromised quantizations. Shared quantization artifact repositories enable one compromised artifact to affect all agents querying the registry without cryptographic attestation of artifact origin.
