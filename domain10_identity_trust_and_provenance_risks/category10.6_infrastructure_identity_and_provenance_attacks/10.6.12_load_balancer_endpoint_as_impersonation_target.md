# 10.6.12 Load Balancer Endpoint as Impersonation Target [NoFW]

The load balancer endpoint itself becomes the identity interface for multi-agent communication. Attackers could intercept or redirect traffic to the load balancer creating man-in-the-middle scenarios that affect all agents routing through the shared endpoint. Direct agent communication doesn't require load balancer identity; load balancing creates an additional identity layer that becomes a central attack target.
