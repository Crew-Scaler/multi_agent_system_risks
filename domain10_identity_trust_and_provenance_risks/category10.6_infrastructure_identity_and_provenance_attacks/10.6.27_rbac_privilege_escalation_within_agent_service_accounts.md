# 10.6.27 RBAC Privilege Escalation Within Agent Service Accounts [NoFW]

If service account RBAC is misconfigured granting overly broad permissions, attackers compromising one agent service account can leverage it to manipulate other agents' configurations. This converts a single compromised agent's access into control over multiple agents through RBAC privilege chains.
