# 10.6.16 Inter-Service Communication Authentication Bypass via TLS Downgrade [NoFW]

Microservices authenticate through mTLS certificates, but attackers exploiting container orchestration misconfiguration can force service-to-service communication to downgrade to HTTP, removing authentication and enabling man-in-the-middle attacks between agents. The identity verification layer is eliminated for all inter-agent communication traversing the downgraded path.
