# 12.2.74 Policy Output Distribution Analysis as Monitoring [NoFW]

Learning-based policies' output distributions reveal policy characteristics enabling anomaly detection of adversarial influence; however, sophisticated adversaries train policies matching benign distributions while embedding hidden triggers, and in multi-agent systems synchronized policy distributions reveal coordinated attacks that are harder to distinguish from benign collective behavior changes because multi-agent averaging obscures individual policy manipulation.
