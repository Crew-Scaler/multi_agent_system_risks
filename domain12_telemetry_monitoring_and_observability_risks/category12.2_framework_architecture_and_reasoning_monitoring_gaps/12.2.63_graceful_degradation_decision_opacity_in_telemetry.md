# 12.2.63 Graceful Degradation Decision Opacity in Telemetry [NoFW]

Degradation telemetry shows which capabilities degraded but not why specific degradation decisions were made; in multi-agent systems, degradation decisions influenced by poisoned component health data appear as legitimate responses to failures, and telemetry lacking degradation decision reasoning cannot detect attacks manipulating component health signals to trigger unintended degradation across agent capabilities.
