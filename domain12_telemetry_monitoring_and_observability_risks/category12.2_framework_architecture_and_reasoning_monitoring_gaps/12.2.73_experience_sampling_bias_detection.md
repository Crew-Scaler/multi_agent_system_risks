# 12.2.73 Experience Sampling Bias Detection [NoFW]

Deep reinforcement learning systems sample from experience buffers in patterns that reveal which experiences are prioritized, and monitoring these sampling patterns can in principle indicate which training focus areas are being manipulated; however, in multi-agent systems aggregated sampling across agents reveals aggregated training focus that obscures individual agent manipulation, and sophisticated adversaries exploit this by introducing sampling bias that averages out in aggregate telemetry.
