# 12.2.66 Weight-Driven Decision Divergence Blind Spot [NoFW]

When multi-agent systems use different utility weights, decision divergence appears as normal analytical variation rather than indicating attacks; monitoring cannot detect that divergence results from injected weight differences rather than legitimate differences, because per-agent monitoring sees consistent individual behavior while the cross-agent weight inconsistency is only visible through comparative analysis that standard monitoring does not perform.
