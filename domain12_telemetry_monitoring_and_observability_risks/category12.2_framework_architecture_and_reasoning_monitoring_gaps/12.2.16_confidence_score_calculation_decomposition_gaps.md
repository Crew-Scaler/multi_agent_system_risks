# 12.2.16 Confidence Score Calculation Decomposition Gaps [NoFW]

Implicit confidence scores derived from model probability distributions are logged as final scalar values without capturing which aspects contributed, making manipulation of the underlying probability space appear as random natural variation rather than a targeted attack.
