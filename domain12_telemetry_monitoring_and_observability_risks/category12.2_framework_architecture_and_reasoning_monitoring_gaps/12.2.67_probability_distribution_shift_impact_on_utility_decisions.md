# 12.2.67 Probability Distribution Shift Impact on Utility Decisions [NoFW]

Monitoring lacks visibility into how outcome probability distribution changes affect expected utility calculations; when probabilities shift due to market conditions, tool reliability degradation, or adversarial manipulation, monitoring continues tracking decision frequency without detecting that utility-calculation-fundamental changes have occurred, and in multi-agent systems probability distribution shifts can cause cascading recalculations across agent networks that monitoring does not correlate.
