# 12.2.52 Search Tree Expansion Rate Blinding [NoFW]

Monitoring systems alert on sudden increases in A* search tree expansion rates as a proxy for anomalous planning load; adversaries degrade heuristic quality incrementally to expand search trees gradually, staying within per-alert thresholds at individual agent level while aggregated multi-agent metrics obscure the compound degradation through averaging with unaffected agents.
