# 12.2.48 Convergence Failures as Unmonitored Planning Breakdowns [NoFW]

When MCTS fails to converge to a reasonable strategy due to budget exhaustion, miscalibrated exploration, or corrupted value functions, planning quality silently degrades without triggering any monitoring alert; downstream agents that depend on the upstream agent's planning output inherit degraded plans with no indication of the root cause.

## Supporting Evidence

- Muhammad Khalifa et al.. 2026. Process Reward Models That Think. *Transactions on Machine Learning Research (TMLR)*. https://openreview.net/forum?id=FPVCb0WMuN
