# 12.2.60 Retry Sequence Telemetry Missing Attack Indicators [NoFW]

Retry telemetry records retry counts and success rates but fails to capture retry sequence characteristics indicating attacks; in multi-agent systems, specific retry patterns such as rapid-succession retries, retries on non-retryable errors, or simultaneous retries from multiple agents may indicate attacks, but standard telemetry recording "operation retried 3 times then succeeded" misses the detailed sequence revealing attack patterns.
