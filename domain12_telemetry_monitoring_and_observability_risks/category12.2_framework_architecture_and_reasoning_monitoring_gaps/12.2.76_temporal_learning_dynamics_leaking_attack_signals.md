# 12.2.76 Temporal Learning Dynamics Leaking Attack Signals [NoFW]

Learning curves (loss, reward progression) can leak attack signatures because poisoned training shows distinctive convergence patterns; monitoring learning curves can detect training-time attacks, but slow poisoning spread over many iterations hides attack signatures in noise, and in multi-agent systems aggregated learning dynamics across agents further hide individual attack signals by averaging poisoned convergence with unaffected agents' normal curves.
