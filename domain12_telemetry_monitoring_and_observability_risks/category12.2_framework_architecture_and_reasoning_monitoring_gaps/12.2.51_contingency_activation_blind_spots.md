# 12.2.51 Contingency Activation Blind Spots [NoFW]

Monitoring distinguishes between unexpected and expected contingency activations, treating activations in response to detected failures as benign; adversaries who corrupt contingency branch conditions to trigger activations under false pretenses exploit this monitoring assumption, as synchronized contingency use across agents appears identical whether driven by real failures or attacker-controlled triggers.
