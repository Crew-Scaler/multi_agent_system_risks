# 12.2.41 Method Selection Monitoring Lacking Failure Prediction [NoFW]

Monitoring logs which HTN method was selected and executed but does not flag when a safer alternative method was available and not chosen; suboptimal method selections proceed without monitoring alerts unless the chosen method actually fails.
