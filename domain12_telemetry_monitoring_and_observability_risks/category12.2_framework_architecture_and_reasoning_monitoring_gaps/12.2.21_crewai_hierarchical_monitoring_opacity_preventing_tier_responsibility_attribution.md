# 12.2.21 CrewAI Hierarchical Monitoring Opacity Preventing Tier Responsibility Attribution [NoFW]

Multi-tier CrewAI hierarchies log which management level issued instructions but do not capture sufficient context to determine which tier introduced corruption, preventing forensic attribution of poisoned outputs to a specific responsible agent.
