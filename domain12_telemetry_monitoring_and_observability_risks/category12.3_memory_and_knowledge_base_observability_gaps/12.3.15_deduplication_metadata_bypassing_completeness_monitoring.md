# 12.3.15 Deduplication Metadata Bypassing Completeness Monitoring [NoFW]

Deduplication processes track how many documents were removed as duplicates but not which specific documents were considered duplicates of which source, preventing detection of false-positive deduplication that removes unique legitimate content or false-negative deduplication that allows poisoned documents to persist alongside their originals.
