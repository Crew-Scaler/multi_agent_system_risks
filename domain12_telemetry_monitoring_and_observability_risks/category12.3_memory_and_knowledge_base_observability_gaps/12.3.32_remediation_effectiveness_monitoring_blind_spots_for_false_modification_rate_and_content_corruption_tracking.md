# 12.3.32 Remediation Effectiveness Monitoring Blind Spots for False Modification Rate and Content Corruption Tracking [NoFW]

Data remediation monitoring tracks counts of applied remediations (PII redactions, spam removals, format normalizations) without tracking false positive rates or confidence score distributions; over-aggressive remediation that removes legitimate content appears as high remediation activity rather than content corruption, and per-agent remediation quality differences are masked in aggregate counts.
