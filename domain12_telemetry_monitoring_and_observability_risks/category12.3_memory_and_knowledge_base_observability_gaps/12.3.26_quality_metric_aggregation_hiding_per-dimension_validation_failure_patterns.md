# 12.3.26 Quality Metric Aggregation Hiding Per-Dimension Validation Failure Patterns [NoFW]

Aggregate quality scores that combine completeness, accuracy, validity, and consistency dimensions mask per-dimension and per-source failure patterns; operators cannot determine from an aggregate score whether degradation stems from completeness issues, accuracy issues, or validity issues, preventing targeted remediation of specific quality failure modes.
