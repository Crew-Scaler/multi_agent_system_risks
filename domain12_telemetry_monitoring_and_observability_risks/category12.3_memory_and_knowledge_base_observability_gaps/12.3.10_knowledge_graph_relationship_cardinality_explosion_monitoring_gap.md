# 12.3.10 Knowledge Graph Relationship Cardinality Explosion Monitoring Gap [NoFW]

Knowledge graph inference rules can create exponential relationship counts in subgraphs; monitoring that tracks total relationship counts may not detect subgraph cardinality explosions until system-wide performance degradation becomes apparent, by which point all agents sharing the graph are already affected.
