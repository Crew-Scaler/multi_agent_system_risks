# 12.3.28 Deduplication Effectiveness Tracking Blind Spots Masking False Positive Inflation and Unique Content Loss [NoFW]

Deduplication monitoring tracks duplicate removal counts and aggregate rates without tracking false positive rates; when deduplication marks unique legitimate documents as duplicates and removes them, monitoring shows increased deduplication effectiveness (higher removal rate) while actual effect is content loss, and the absence of falsely-removed documents from the knowledge base goes undetected until users report missing information.
