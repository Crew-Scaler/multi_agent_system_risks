# 12.4.15 Streaming Rate Limiting Evasion Through Distribution [NoFW]

Rate limiting on streaming endpoints is evaded in multi-agent systems by distributing expensive streaming content across multiple concurrent agents, each individually below per-agent rate limits while aggregate consumption exceeds system capacity; unlike singular streaming endpoints with straightforward rate limiting, multi-agent streaming requires cross-agent coordination for aggregate rate enforcement, and monitoring N concurrent streaming agents for compound limit violations creates operational complexity that attackers exploit through careful distribution timing.
