# 12.4.20 Demonstration Ordering as Covert Instruction Activation Mechanism [NoFW]

In-context learning demonstrations are susceptible to ordering attacks that exploit recency bias; adversaries strategically position malicious instruction examples at the end of demonstration sequences to maximize their influence on agent behavior, and monitoring systems focused on explicit instruction injection miss ordering-based control flow manipulation that activates malicious behavior through positional context.
