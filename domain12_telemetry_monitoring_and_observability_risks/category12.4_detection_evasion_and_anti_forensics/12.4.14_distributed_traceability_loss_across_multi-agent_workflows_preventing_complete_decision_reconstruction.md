# 12.4.14 Distributed Traceability Loss Across Multi-Agent Workflows Preventing Complete Decision Reconstruction [NoFW]

Multi-agent workflow decisions are logged in per-agent databases with independent schemas, different retention periods, and without explicit causal linkage between agent handoffs; regulatory compliance investigations that require complete decision reconstruction cannot achieve the audit trail coverage needed to demonstrate that protected characteristics played no role in adverse decisions when 30–50% of the decision chain is unavailable due to retention policy deletions, schema incompatibilities, and missing cross-agent causal links.
