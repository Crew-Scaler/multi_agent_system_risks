# 12.5.35 Cross-Agent Trajectory Metrics Rarely Computed [NoFW]

Trajectory evaluation metrics (exact match, precision, recall over complete action sequences) require complete action sequences spanning multiple agents' concurrent and asynchronous operations, but are almost never computed in production; without trajectory-level metrics, action sequence quality across agent chains is unmonitored, and degradation in multi-agent coordination quality is only detected through eventual user-impact reports.
