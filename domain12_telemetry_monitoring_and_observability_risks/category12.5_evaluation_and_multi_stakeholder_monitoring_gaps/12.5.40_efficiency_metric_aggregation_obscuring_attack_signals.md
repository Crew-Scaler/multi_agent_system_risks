# 12.5.40 Efficiency Metric Aggregation Obscuring Attack Signals [NoFW]

Efficiency metrics aggregated across multiple agents and operations obscure per-agent or per-operation attack signals that would be detectable at finer granularity; a 0.1% efficiency degradation from a targeted agent-level attack disappears in fleet-wide aggregate metrics, making only large-scale or simultaneous attacks detectable through aggregated efficiency monitoring.
