# 12.5.103 Streaming Progress Indicator Manipulation for Activity Obfuscation [NoFW]

Streaming progress indicators showing content delivery rates can be manipulated by attackers controlling streaming speed to hide expensive background operations; slow streaming of benign content while rapid internal processing of malicious operations creates the illusion that visible progress is aligned with actual system activity, and in multi-agent monitoring that observes visible streaming progress, background processing by concurrent agents remains invisible behind the deceptively-slow visible stream.
