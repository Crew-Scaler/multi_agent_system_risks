# 12.5.109 Demonstration Curation Audit Trail Opacity [NoFW]

Few-shot demonstration selection lacks audit trails clearly showing which examples were selected, when, and why; in multi-agent systems with shared demonstration pools, attackers who poison demonstration curation affect all agents pulling from the shared pool, but audit trails show only "demonstration pool changed" rather than tracing per-agent demonstration selection accountability, preventing attribution of poisoned in-context learning behavior to the specific curation manipulation.
