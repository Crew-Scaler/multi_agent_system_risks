# 12.5.38 Reasoning Quality Evaluation Scope Limitations [NoFW]

Reasoning quality evaluation frameworks test happy-path reasoning scenarios but not reasoning quality under error conditions, unusual inputs, or adversarial perturbations; adversaries who discover that reasoning quality collapses under specific out-of-distribution conditions can exploit these untested blind spots knowing that monitoring has no baseline for detecting reasoning collapse in error scenarios.
