# 12.5.39 Attribution and Traceability Gaps in Reasoning Chains [NoFW]

Multi-agent systems lack mechanisms to trace reasoning contributions back to specific agents; when emergent reasoning failures occur at agent handoffs, the absence of per-agent reasoning attribution prevents identification of which agent introduced the reasoning error, enabling adversaries who inject corrupted reasoning into intermediate agents to hide in attribution ambiguity.
