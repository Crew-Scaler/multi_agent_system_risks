# 12.5.68 Alert Rule Suppression Through Metric Threshold Manipulation [NoFW]

Adversaries who can influence metrics from specific agents can manipulate those agents' metrics to stay just below alert thresholds while other agents experience cascading effects; alert rules based on manipulated agent metrics remain silent while legitimate impact propagates to agents whose metrics the adversary did not directly control.
