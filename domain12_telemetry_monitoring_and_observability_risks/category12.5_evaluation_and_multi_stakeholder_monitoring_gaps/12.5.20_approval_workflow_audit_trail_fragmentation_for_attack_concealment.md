# 12.5.20 Approval Workflow Audit Trail Fragmentation for Attack Concealment [NoFW]

Multi-agent approval workflows fragment audit trails across per-agent log streams at each agent handoff boundary; attacks that span the approval chain (manipulating the recommendation agent's output, the approval agent's decision, and the execution agent's action) leave evidence distributed across three separate logs with no unified audit trail connecting the manipulated chain.
