# 12.5.84 Engine Metadata Drift Undetectable Without Binary Inspection [NoFW]

TensorRT engine binaries are opaque at runtime; monitoring systems cannot detect filesystem-level tampering with cached engine files unless explicit binary hashes are computed and compared, and in multi-agent fleets with hundreds of cached engines across many edge locations, comprehensive continuous binary validation is operationally infeasible at scale.
