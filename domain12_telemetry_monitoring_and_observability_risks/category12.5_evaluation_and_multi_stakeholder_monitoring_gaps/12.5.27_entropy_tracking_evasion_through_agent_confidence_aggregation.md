# 12.5.27 Entropy Tracking Evasion Through Agent Confidence Aggregation [NoFW]

Hallucination detection based on generation entropy is defeated when a high-confidence downstream validation agent reviews and affirms uncertain upstream generation; aggregate confidence in multi-agent systems can appear healthy when downstream validators consistently affirm uncertain upstream outputs, masking the uncertainty that entropy monitoring is designed to detect.
