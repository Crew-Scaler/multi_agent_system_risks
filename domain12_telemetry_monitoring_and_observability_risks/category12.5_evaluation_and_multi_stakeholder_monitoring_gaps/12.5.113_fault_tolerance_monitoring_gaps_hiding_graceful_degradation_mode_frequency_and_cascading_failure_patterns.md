# 12.5.113 Fault Tolerance Monitoring Gaps Hiding Graceful Degradation Mode Frequency and Cascading Failure Patterns [NoFW]

Circuit breaker monitoring reports current state (open or closed) without tracking how frequently circuits open, how long degraded modes persist, or whether one circuit opening causally triggers others; operators see a closed circuit and assume healthy operation while missing that the circuit oscillates open/closed dozens of times daily or that cascading circuit failures occur across dependent services.
