# 12.5.69 Audit Log Asynchronous Write Latency Creating Compliance Blind Spots [NoFW]

Audit logs written asynchronously may be lost if agents crash before buffers flush; adversaries who can trigger agent crashes immediately after malicious operations prevent those operations from entering the audit trail, and in multi-agent systems where coordinated crashes prevent multiple agents' audit entries from being written, coordinated attacks can be executed with minimal or no audit evidence.
