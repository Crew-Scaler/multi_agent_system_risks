# 12.5.78 Inference Latency Spikes Masked by Batching Indeterminism [NoFW]

Dynamic batching in multi-agent Triton deployments creates legitimate latency variance from non-deterministic batch compositions; adversaries who craft inference payloads that marginally increase latency exploit this variance to hide their impact within normal batching noise, and monitoring thresholds calibrated to batch variance rather than individual request processing time cannot detect the adversarial contribution.
