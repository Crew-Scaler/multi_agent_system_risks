# 12.5.42 Latency Percentile Reporting Masking Attack Spikes [NoFW]

Efficiency monitoring reports latency at p50, p95, and p99 percentiles; attack-induced latency spikes affecting only 0.5% of operations fall below the p99 reporting threshold and remain invisible in standard percentile dashboards, requiring p99.5 or maximum latency monitoring that is typically not configured.
