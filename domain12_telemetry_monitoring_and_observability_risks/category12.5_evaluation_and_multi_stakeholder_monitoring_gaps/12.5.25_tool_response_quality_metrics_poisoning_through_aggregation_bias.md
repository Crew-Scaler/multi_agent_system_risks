# 12.5.25 Tool Response Quality Metrics Poisoning Through Aggregation Bias [NoFW]

Multi-agent systems aggregate tool quality metrics (success rates, latency) across agents; adversaries compromise a subset of agents to report inflated quality metrics, skewing aggregate metrics that drive tool retirement and selection decisions toward attacker-preferred outcomes while individual per-agent quality analysis would reveal the manipulation.
