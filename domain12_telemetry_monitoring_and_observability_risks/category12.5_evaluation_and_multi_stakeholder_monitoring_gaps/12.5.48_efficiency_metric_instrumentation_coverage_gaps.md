# 12.5.48 Efficiency Metric Instrumentation Coverage Gaps [NoFW]

Not all operations are instrumented for efficiency metrics; adversaries identify under-instrumented agents or code paths and route malicious operations through those paths knowing they will not appear in efficiency monitoring; in multi-agent systems, instrumentation coverage heterogeneity across agents enables selective attack routing through least-monitored agents.
