# 12.5.41 Sampling-Based Efficiency Monitoring Creating Blind Spots [NoFW]

High-volume systems monitor efficiency metrics through sampling (1% of operations), allowing adversaries to craft attacks that trigger only under conditions not covered by the sampling strategy; malicious efficiency behaviors present in the unsampled 99% of operations remain undetected indefinitely.
