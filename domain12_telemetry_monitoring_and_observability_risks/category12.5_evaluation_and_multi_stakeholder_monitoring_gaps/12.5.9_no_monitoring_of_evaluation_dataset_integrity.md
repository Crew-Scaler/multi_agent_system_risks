# 12.5.9 No Monitoring of Evaluation Dataset Integrity [NoFW]

Evaluation datasets accessed by evaluation agents in multi-agent systems are not monitored for content modifications; adversaries who poison evaluation datasets leave no audit trail detectable through evaluation pipeline monitoring because datasets are treated as trusted static inputs rather than as mutable attack surfaces requiring integrity verification.
