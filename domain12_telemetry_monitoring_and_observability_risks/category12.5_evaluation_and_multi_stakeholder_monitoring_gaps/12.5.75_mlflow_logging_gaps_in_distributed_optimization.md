# 12.5.75 MLflow Logging Gaps in Distributed Optimization [NoFW]

Optimization decisions propagated through agent-to-agent communication channels are not logged to MLflow if agents do not explicitly call logging APIs for each communicated decision; in distributed multi-agent optimization where coordination occurs through message passing rather than centralized decision-making, entire optimization dimensions remain outside MLflow's observability coverage.
