# 12.5.37 Reasoning Quality Drift in Production Without Continuous Monitoring [NoFW]

Reasoning quality evaluation is performed pre-deployment but not maintained as a continuous production metric; gradual reasoning quality degradation from model updates, prompt drift, or distribution shift goes undetected until it manifests as user-facing failures, and in multi-agent systems this degradation cascades through downstream agents, amplifying its impact beyond what single-agent reasoning drift would produce.
