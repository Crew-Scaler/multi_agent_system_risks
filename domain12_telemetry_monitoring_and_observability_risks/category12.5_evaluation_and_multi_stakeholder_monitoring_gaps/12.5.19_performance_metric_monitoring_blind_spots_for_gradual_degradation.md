# 12.5.19 Performance Metric Monitoring Blind Spots for Gradual Degradation [NoFW]

Per-agent performance metrics are monitored independently; coordinated attacks that degrade multiple agents' calibration simultaneously by small amounts create compound quality degradation that exceeds acceptable system-level bounds while no single agent's individual degradation exceeds its per-agent alert threshold.
