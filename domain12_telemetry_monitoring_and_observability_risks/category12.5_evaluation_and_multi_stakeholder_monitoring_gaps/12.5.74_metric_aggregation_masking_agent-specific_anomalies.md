# 12.5.74 Metric Aggregation Masking Agent-Specific Anomalies [NoFW]

Fleet-wide metric averages for latency, throughput, and error rates mask individual agent anomalies through averaging effects; a compromised agent exhibiting injection-driven anomalous behavior remains undetected when its metrics deviate within the natural variance range of the fleet average, enabling long-term undetected compromise for agents operating in large heterogeneous fleets.
