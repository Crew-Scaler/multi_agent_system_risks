# 12.5.44 Efficiency Alert Thresholds Missing Gradual Degradation [NoFW]

Discrete alert thresholds detect sudden efficiency degradation but not gradual degradation that stays within threshold bounds; adversaries cause slow cumulative resource exhaustion (token consumption growing from 3K to 9K over hours) without triggering discrete alerts, resulting in budget exhaustion that appears as a sudden event to monitoring systems that track only current threshold crossings.
