# 12.5.77 Profiling Data Retention and Forensic Gaps [NoFW]

GPU and CPU profiling data is retained briefly due to storage constraints, creating windows where attacks occurring between profiling periods leave no forensic evidence; in multi-agent systems where profiling decisions are made at fleet level, adversaries who understand the profiling schedule can time attacks to occur between profiling windows across all agents simultaneously.
