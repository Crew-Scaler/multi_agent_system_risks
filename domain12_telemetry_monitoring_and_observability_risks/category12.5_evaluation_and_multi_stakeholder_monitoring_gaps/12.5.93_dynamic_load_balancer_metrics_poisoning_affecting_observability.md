# 12.5.93 Dynamic Load Balancer Metrics Poisoning Affecting Observability [NoFW]

Dynamic load balancers expose health metrics that guide routing decisions and feed into monitoring dashboards; adversaries who can manipulate these health metrics corrupt both routing behavior and the observability layer that monitoring teams use to assess fleet health, creating coordinated confusion between what is happening and what monitoring reports.
