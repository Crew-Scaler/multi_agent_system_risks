# 12.5.92 Auto-Scaling Configuration Blind Spots [NoFW]

Auto-scaling adds new agent replicas with potentially different monitoring configurations than the existing fleet; newly provisioned replicas may lack telemetry collectors, have different logging levels, or be missing alert rules that the baseline fleet has, creating monitoring coverage inconsistencies that grow with fleet scale and provide adversaries with under-monitored entry points.
