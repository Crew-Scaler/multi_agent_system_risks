# 12.5.85 Quantization Artifact Telemetry Absence [NoFW]

Production monitoring does not collect quantization-specific metrics such as activation range distributions, per-layer quantization error accumulation, or KV cache integrity indicators; entire attack classes that operate through quantization precision manipulation generate no monitored telemetry and therefore have zero detection probability through standard observability infrastructure.
