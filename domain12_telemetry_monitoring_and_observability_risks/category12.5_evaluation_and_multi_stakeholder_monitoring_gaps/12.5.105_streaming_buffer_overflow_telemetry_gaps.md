# 12.5.105 Streaming Buffer Overflow Telemetry Gaps [NoFW]

Streaming implementations with fixed buffers overflow when streamed content exceeds buffer capacity, and overflow telemetry typically logs the fact of overflow without capturing the overflowed content (which was discarded); in multi-agent systems, attackers deliberately stream large content payloads to exploit buffer overflow, discarding malicious payload while telemetry shows only that overflow occurred without recording what was lost, and multi-agent buffer overflows across multiple channels create distributed content-loss patterns difficult to correlate into a complete attack picture.
