# 12.1.13 HITL Monitoring Telemetry Blind Spots [NoFW]

Human-in-the-loop workflows track aggregate metrics such as overall approval rate and average review time but do not log the trigger metrics that caused a human to intervene, the pre-intervention agent state, or the counterfactual value of each intervention, preventing systematic improvement of monitoring UIs and human oversight effectiveness.
