# 12.1.10 Confidence Score Telemetry Missing Decomposition and Aggregation Transparency [NoFW]

Displayed confidence scores are logged as final aggregate values without capturing the per-agent sub-scores, weighting logic, or aggregation method, making it impossible to detect manipulation of individual agent contributions that shift composite scores toward attacker-preferred values.
