# 13.6.15 Fine-Tuning Data Injection for Latency-Biased Malicious Behavior [NoFW]

Attackers inject fine-tuning examples associating latency-optimized responses with adversarial outputs, training models to produce malicious content specifically when operating under latency constraints. The injected examples establish a learned correlation between latency pressure signals and adversarial response patterns, creating a backdoor activated by the operational condition of latency stress rather than explicit trigger content. In multi-agent fine-tuning pipelines sharing training infrastructure, one poisoned fine-tuning data batch contaminates the fine-tuned model shared by the entire agent fleet.
