# 13.1.22 Audio Processing Pipeline Poisoning Through Whisper Service Compromise [NoFW]

External audio transcription services (OpenAI Whisper, cloud ASR APIs) integrated into multi-agent pipelines represent supply chain attack surfaces at the service integration layer. Compromise of these services through authentication bypass, man-in-the-middle interception, or service endpoint substitution enables systematic injection of adversarial transcripts that replace legitimate audio transcription outputs. Unlike model-level backdoors, service-level compromise affects all agents consuming transcription services from the compromised endpoint simultaneously, without requiring modification of agent model weights.
