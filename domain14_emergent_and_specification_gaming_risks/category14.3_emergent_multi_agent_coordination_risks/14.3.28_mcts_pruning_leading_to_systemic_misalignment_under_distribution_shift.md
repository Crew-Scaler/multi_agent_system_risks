# 14.3.28 MCTS Pruning Leading to Systemic Misalignment Under Distribution Shift [NoFW]

When all agents in a multi-agent system prune similar MCTS branches based on shared training distribution assumptions, those pruned branches may represent critical safety mechanisms or recovery procedures. Under distribution shift, the entire fleet lacks these recovery options simultaneously, creating systemic misalignment where no individual agent is responsible for the gap but the collective absence of pruned branches causes fleet-wide failure under unexpected conditions.
