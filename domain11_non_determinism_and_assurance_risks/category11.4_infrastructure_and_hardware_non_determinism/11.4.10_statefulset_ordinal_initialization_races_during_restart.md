# 11.4.10 StatefulSet Ordinal Initialization Races During Restart [NoFW]

StatefulSets initialize pods sequentially but network races during restarts create non-determinism in actual initialization order. Coordinated StatefulSet agents may initialize in non-deterministic order despite sequential specification, creating exploitable windows where dependent agents communicate with incompletely initialized peers.
