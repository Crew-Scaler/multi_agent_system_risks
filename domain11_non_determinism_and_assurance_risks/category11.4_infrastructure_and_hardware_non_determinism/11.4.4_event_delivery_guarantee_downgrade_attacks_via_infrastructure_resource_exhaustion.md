# 11.4.4 Event Delivery Guarantee Downgrade Attacks via Infrastructure Resource Exhaustion [NoFW]

Multi-agent event-driven systems rely on exactly-once or at-least-once delivery infrastructure guarantees. Attackers exhaust resources forcing silent degradation to weaker guarantees, enabling duplicate or lost operations (e.g., duplicate financial transactions) that the application layer cannot detect.
