# 11.4.18 Profiling-Induced Non-Determinism as Assurance Violation [NoFW]

Profiling instrumentation adds measurement overhead that perturbs execution timing and scheduling decisions, creating Heisenbug-like behavior where profiled execution differs fundamentally from uninstrumented baseline. Attackers design attacks that manifest only in non-profiled production execution, evading detection during instrumented security analysis.
