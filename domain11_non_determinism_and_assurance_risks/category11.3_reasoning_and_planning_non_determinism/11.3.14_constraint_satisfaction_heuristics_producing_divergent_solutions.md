# 11.3.14 Constraint Satisfaction Heuristics Producing Divergent Solutions [NoFW]

HTN constraint satisfaction has multiple valid solutions and heuristics choosing among them are non-deterministic. Multi-agent systems where solving and execution separate enable unexpected constraint solutions that were not evaluated during safety testing.
