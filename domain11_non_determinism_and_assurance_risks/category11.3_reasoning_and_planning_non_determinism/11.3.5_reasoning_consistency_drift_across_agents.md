# 11.3.5 Reasoning Consistency Drift Across Agents [NoFW]

As agents update individually their reasoning patterns diverge. A poisoning attack exploiting one agent's reasoning pattern may fail on updated agents but succeed on unupdated peers, creating persistent drift-dependent exploitability across the multi-agent fleet.
