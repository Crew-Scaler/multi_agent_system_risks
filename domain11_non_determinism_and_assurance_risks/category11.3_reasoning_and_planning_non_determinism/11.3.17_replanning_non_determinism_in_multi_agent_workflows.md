# 11.3.17 Replanning Non-Determinism in Multi-Agent Workflows [NoFW]

MCTS replanning generates new plans when execution fails. Replanning from identical failure states with different random seeds produces different recovery plans, and attackers exploit this by forcing replanning where specific recovery paths execute dangerous operations.
