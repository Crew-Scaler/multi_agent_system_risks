# 11.3.16 Simulation Budget Variance as Assurance Gap [NoFW]

MCTS quality depends on simulation budget but real deployments have variable budgets due to multi-agent resource competition. Reduced budgets under production load produce lower-quality plans that diverge from safety-tested high-budget plans, creating exploitable assurance gaps.
