# 11.3.15 Dynamic MCTS Adaptation Enabling Non-Deterministic Attacks [NoFW]

Adaptive MCTS systems tune exploration constant C dynamically. Agents with higher C values sample more alternatives and are disproportionately susceptible to injected adversarial content encountered during expanded tree search, while lower-C agents converge quickly and miss the injection.
