# 11.5.9 RAG Result Ranking Non-Determinism Due to Score Rounding [NoFW]

Document similarity scores undergo rounding producing non-deterministic ranking boundaries near score ties. Shared ranking functions create correlated non-determinism across all agents, enabling adversarial documents crafted to score near the boundary to retrieve intermittently and unpredictably.
