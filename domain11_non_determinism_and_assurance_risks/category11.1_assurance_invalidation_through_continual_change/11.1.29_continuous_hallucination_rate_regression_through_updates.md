# 11.1.29 Continuous Hallucination Rate Regression Through Updates [NoFW]

Agent updates through fine-tuning regress hallucination rates unpredictably. Grounding checks calibrated to pre-update rates become ineffective when regression increases hallucination past calibrated thresholds, creating post-update exploitation windows.
