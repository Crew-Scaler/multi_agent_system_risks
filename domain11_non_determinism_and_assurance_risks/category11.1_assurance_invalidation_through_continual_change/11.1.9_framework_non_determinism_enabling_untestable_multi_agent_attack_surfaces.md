# 11.1.9 Framework Non-Determinism Enabling Untestable Multi-Agent Attack Surfaces [NoFW]

Different MAS frameworks introduce non-determinism through different mechanisms (LangChain temperature, LangGraph reducers, AutoGen randomness). Combining frameworks creates multiplicative non-determinism where attack success varies by 30.8–52.3% across frameworks, making comprehensive testing infeasible.
