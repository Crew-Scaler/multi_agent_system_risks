# 11.1.15 Evaluation Pipeline Component Drift Through Continual Updates [NoFW]

Multi-stage evaluation pipelines have components updating independently. Component drift creates inconsistency making results non-reproducible across evaluations, invalidating cumulative assurance records and audit trails.
