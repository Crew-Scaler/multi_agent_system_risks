# 11.6.5 Stochastic Outcome Distribution Changes Breaking Cached Utility Calculations [NoFW]

Agents caching expected utility values face invalidation when outcome distributions change post-caching. Cached utilities remain in use despite becoming invalid, and multi-agent cascading propagates one agent's stale utility assumptions through dependent agents.
