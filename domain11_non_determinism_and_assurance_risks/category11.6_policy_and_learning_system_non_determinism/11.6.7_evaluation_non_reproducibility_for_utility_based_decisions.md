# 11.6.7 Evaluation Non-Reproducibility for Utility-Based Decisions [NoFW]

Agents making decisions through expected utility optimization face non-reproducible testing. Testing reveals safe decisions under one sampled distribution, but identical future inputs trigger unsafe decisions when the utility-sampling distribution shifts, making pre-deployment safety evaluation unreliable for utility-based agents.
