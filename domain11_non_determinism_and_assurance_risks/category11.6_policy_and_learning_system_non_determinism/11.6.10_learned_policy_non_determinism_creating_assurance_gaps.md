# 11.6.10 Learned Policy Non-Determinism Creating Assurance Gaps [NoFW]

Learning-based policies contain stochastic components (softmax action selection, dropout, temperature sampling). Identical states produce different actions, and multi-agent policy non-determinism compounds creating exponential behavior variance that makes comprehensive pre-deployment assurance infeasible.
