# 11.6.13 Policy Network Weight Sensitivity to Training Details [NoFW]

Learned policy weights depend sensitively on training details. Identical architectures trained with different random seeds, data orderings, or hardware produce different policies, and distributed multi-agent training with heterogeneous execution produces a fleet of heterogeneous policies with inconsistent security behaviors.
