# 11.6.6 Non-Deterministic Weight Adjustment Mechanisms Creating Unsafe Adaptation [NoFW]

Systems dynamically adjusting utility weights based on outcomes face non-deterministic convergence. Multi-agent systems enable divergent convergence where agents develop incompatible utility functions, creating collective decision-making behavior that no single agent's evaluation could predict.
