# 11.6.12 Experience Replay Temporal Non-Determinism [NoFW]

Deep RL systems sample experiences from replay buffers with temporal gaps, causing the same state to produce different policy updates depending on which historical experiences are sampled. Shared replay buffers create correlated temporal non-determinism across all agents drawing from the same experience pool.
