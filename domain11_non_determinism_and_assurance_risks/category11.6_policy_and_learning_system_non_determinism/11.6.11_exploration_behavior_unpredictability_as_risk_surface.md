# 11.6.11 Exploration Behavior Unpredictability as Risk Surface [NoFW]

Learning systems maintain exploration phases even during deployment. Inherent unpredictability of exploration creates assurance gaps where safety evaluation cannot guarantee dangerous actions will never be explored in production. Coordinated multi-agent exploration creates correlated unpredictability amplifying the risk surface.
